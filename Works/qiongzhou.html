Romance of Qiong Zhou, 2016

Description:
A summer, an island, and a companion all became a memory. This was our private time together, employing stream of consciousness as a narrative method to record: beads of water which reflecting the flow of time, agitation felt in the waves which seemed to reveal the myriad of thoughts swirling around within one's mind, swimming nude, an act seemingly free, yet repressed at the same time. Love will turn one into a poet. A poet will recite the canons of love.
