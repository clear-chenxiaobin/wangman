The winding path, 2016

Description:
It was by a matter of chance that I recorded this work. Three kites were flying amidst the gray and cloudy sky. At certain moments, they danced, at others, they gently swayed in the wind. The kites flew together, and then become disordered, displaying a unique kaleidoscopic frame of movement. I became transfixed. The freedom and elegance of the kites made them appear almost as if they were aware-beings. However, at the same time, it also made me feel depressed, as below this expression of freedom was a socalled \"line,\" functioning as a restraint, binding, yet perhaps even more so, an object of fate. In this way, the three kites depict the trajectory of wind and freedom, but also the trajectory of human life.
