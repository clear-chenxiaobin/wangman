Rainbow, 2016

Description:
A rainbow, is a natural phenomenon, but because of its gorgeous and intriguing colors, it has also been an object of interest since ancient times, and thus imbued with varied forms of meaning. Within a confined space, the work showcases a real three-dimensional rainbow, spanning 360 ° , with two rings whose meaning represents death, positioned behind the strong visual effects. I am not interested in transferring a solidified concept, attitude or emotion to the viewer, rather am more interested in reflection. My wish is that the work will evoke a variety of feelings among different viewers.
